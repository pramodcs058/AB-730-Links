## 🧪 Practice Prompts (Learning Paths)

# Knowledge Check Prompts

## LP-1: Generative AI Fundamentals
**Module Link:** https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/training/modules/what-generative-ai/

**Instructions:**
As a generative AI expert, create six multiple-choice questions covering:
- What generative AI is  
- How generative AI works  
- Microsoft’s Responsible AI principles  
- Data privacy and security in Copilot  
- How Copilot uses generative AI to enhance productivity  

**Delivery Guidelines:**
- Present **one question at a time**
- Wait for the learner’s response before proceeding
- After each answer:
  - Provide a **brief explanation** of the correct choice
  - Then move to the next question
